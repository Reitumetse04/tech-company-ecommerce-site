About Us

Founded in 2024 by Jabulani Bakane (CEO), Varsity Store Technologies was born out of a vision to provide accessible, high-quality ICT supplies and IT services to institutions and businesses. What started as a university project has rapidly grown into a trusted technology partner for organizations across South Africa.

We understand that in today’s fast-moving digital world, technology is more than just a tool, it’s the foundation of success. That’s why we’re committed to offering reliable solutions that combine innovation with affordability. Our presence in higher education environments has taught us the importance of agility, efficiency, and personalized service, values that continue to guide us as we grow.

With a growing network of resellers and partners nationwide, we’re positioning ourselves as one of the fastest-rising ICT providers. From supplying hardware and software to delivering customized IT consulting services, we are here to ensure our clients can thrive in an increasingly digital world.

Our Mission

To deliver reliable, affordable, and empowering ICT and business services that help individuals, organizations, and institutions unlock their full potential. We believe in technology that works for people, not the other way around.

Our Vision

To become a nationally recognized technology partner known not only for the solutions we deliver but also for the impact we create — inspiring the next generation of digital entrepreneurs and innovators.

Why Choose Us?

At Varsity Store Technologies, we do more than supply products and services, we build relationships. Our team is driven by a passion for excellence and a commitment to our customers’ long-term success. We combine industry knowledge, affordability, and customer-first solutions to provide value that sets us apart in the market.

Whether you’re a university, business, or individual, partnering with us means gaining a trusted ally who understands your challenges and works with you to achieve real results. Your growth is our mission, and your trust is our greatest reward.
